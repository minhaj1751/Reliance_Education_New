Study in Canada

Canada has developed a first-rate education system with high academic standards that are globally recognized. Canadian institutions offer innovative programs, advanced research facilities, and internationally valued degrees.

One of the major advantages of studying in Canada is the opportunity for permanent residency. International students who complete their studies may qualify for post-study work permits and long-term immigration pathways.

Canada consistently ranks among the top ten countries in the world for quality of life, safety, and student satisfaction.

Why Choose Canada?

Many students struggle with Canadian university and college applications due to strict deadlines. Most institutions do not accept late submissions, which can cause students to miss an intake and wait an entire year.

At Reliance Education, we have been working with Canadian universities and colleges since 2005. Our expert counsellors understand admission timelines, eligibility criteria, and documentation requirements — ensuring a smooth and timely admission process.

Canada Study Visa Process

  • Consultation & Profile Assessment – Evaluation of academic background, finances, and career goals.
  • University & College Application – Institution shortlisting and on-time application submission.
  • Offer Letter & Documentation – GIC, tuition fees, SOP, and sponsor documents preparation.
  • Visa Application Submission – Accurate filing of Canada Study Permit with IRCC.
  • Approval & Pre-departure Support – Travel guidance, accommodation, and orientation.

Types of Canada Visas

  • Student Visa (Study Permit)
  • Post-Graduation Work Permit (PGWP)
  • Visitor Visa
  • Spouse Open Work Permit
  • Permanent Residency (PR Pathways)

Our Canada Study Services

  • Career & Academic Counselling – Course and institution selection based on future goals.
  • SOP & Application Support – Professional documentation and submission assistance.
  • Visa Documentation & Filing – End-to-end study permit support.
  • Financial & Scholarship Guidance – GIC, sponsors, and funding planning.
  • Pre-departure & Post-arrival Support – Accommodation, job tips, and student life guidance.
